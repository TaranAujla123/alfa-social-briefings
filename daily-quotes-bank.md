## HOW TO USE THIS DOC

1. Pick a quote below based on the day's pillar (rotate — don't stack same pillar twice in a week)
2. Copy the headline into your Canva template. **The word in bold + ALL CAPS = the gold accent word**
3. Copy the caption into IG (80–200 words body rule)
4. Append the hashtag set
5. End caption with `One day at a time.`
6. Publish at 7am or 9pm local

**VOICE RULES (never break):**
- ✅ Use: pattern · pull · noise · hard reset · brother · blueprint · mechanism · functioning · dismantle · build · system · the man
- ❌ Never: addict · alcoholic · recovery · healing · journey · therapy · relapse · detox · rehab · "you are not alone" · motivational filler
- ❌ No emojis · No exclamation marks · No "hey guys" openings
